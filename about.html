About Meganest LLC

Backed by USDA Standards. Driven by Family-Owned Values.

Our Story

Meganest LLC was founded in 2025 with a mission to connect families and wholesale buyers across the U.S. to affordable, USDA-certified meat without middlemen or mystery origins.

As a federally recognized wholesaler, we specialize in Whole Lamb and Whole Bison — both high-demand, nutrient-rich meats often hard to find at fair prices. All products are sourced from USDA-licensed partners and shipped with strict cold chain control via FedEx Overnight.

Powered by GrassFedPrime

We proudly operate in partnership with GrassFedPrime, one of the nation’s largest and most trusted USDA-approved meat suppliers. Through this partnership, we guarantee steady supply, bulk order capacity, and full transparency from farm to delivery.

Why It Matters

America deserves access to premium, traceable meat without overpaying brokers or importing frozen inventory. Meganest closes that gap with a legally sound structure, modern eCommerce experience, and responsive order fulfillment — all backed by U.S. law and USDA inspection.

SBA-Ready Business
